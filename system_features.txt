========================================================================
      SRI LANKA FLOOD & LANDSLIDE EARLY WARNING SYSTEM (SLFWS)
                     Features & Capabilities List
========================================================================

1. 🌍 CORE SYSTEM CAPABILITIES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Real-time district-level flood and landslide risk predictions.
- Driven by an XGBoost Machine Learning model.
- Uses NASA GPM IMERG satellite rainfall data and SRTM terrain elevation.
- Seamless Navigation: Home, Methodology, About Us, and Contact Us.

2. 🎛️ SIDEBAR CONTROLS & SETTINGS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Live UTC+5:30 Clock.
- Rainfall Manual Overrides: Adjust current, last month, 2 months ago, and 3 months ago rainfall values (0-600mm) for custom simulations.
- Seasonality Selection: Change the current month to see how monsoon seasons affect risk.
- Alert Preferences: Toggle switches to enable/disable Live Alerts and Sound Alerts.
- System Status Monitor: Real-time status for Satellite Link, Model Inference, Data Pipeline, and API Gateway.

3. 🗺️ 3D RISK MAP (TAB 1)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Interactive 3D extruded map of all 25 districts in Sri Lanka.
- Toggleable Layers: Turn on/off Volumetric Clouds, Animated Rain Drops, Flood Waves (rings), and Landslide Markers.
- Dynamic Elevation: District height reacts visually to the amount of rainfall.
- Color-Coded Risk: Districts change colors from cyan (safe) to crimson (critical) based on AI probability.
- Interactive Tooltips: Hover over any district to see specific Risk %, Rainfall, and Name.

4. 📊 ANALYTICS DASHBOARD (TAB 2)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Risk Level Distribution: Bar chart showing how many districts are Critical, Moderate, or Low risk.
- Top 10 High-Risk Districts: Horizontal bar chart ranking the most vulnerable areas.
- Risk vs Rainfall Scatter Plot: Interactive correlation graph showing risk clusters.
- Average Risk Gauge: Speedometer-style chart showing the national average risk index.
- District Deep Dive (Radar Chart): Select any district to view its specific profile across Overall Risk, Terrain Vulnerability, Rain Intensity, Weather Anomaly, and 3-Month Saturation.

5. 📋 DISTRICT TABLE (TAB 3)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Searchable & Sortable Database: Filter data for specific districts.
- Data Columns: View District, Risk Score, Alert Level, Rainfall (mm), and Terrain Risk at a glance.
- Export Capability: One-click "Download Risk Report" button to save current data as a CSV file.

6. 🚨 ALERTS & INFO (TAB 4)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Live Risk Alerts: Automatically generated text warnings for districts hitting Critical (>=60%) or Moderate (>=35%) risk thresholds.
- Evacuation Guidelines: Expandable safety procedures.
- Emergency Contacts: Quick reference table for DMC Hotline (117), Police, Ambulance, etc.
- Flood Preparedness Tips: Actionable survival information.

7. 🔮 16-DAY FORECAST (TAB 5)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Open-Meteo API Integration: Fetches 16 days of real future weather data.
- Summary Metrics: Displays the Peak Risk District, Peak Risk Date, and total High Risk Days.
- Interactive 2D Spatial Map: Use a slider to scroll through the next 16 days and watch risk (or rainfall) move across Sri Lanka.
- Forecast Heatmap: Visual grid showing all 25 districts against the next 16 days.
- Trend Line: Graph showing the projected national average risk score over the next two weeks.
- Upcoming Critical Events: Automated alert list for any future dates that cross the danger threshold.

8. 📸 VISION AI (TAB 6)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Crowdsourced Ground Truth: Submit field reports to validate the AI.
- Flexible Input: Upload an image file or take a live photo using the device camera.
- CNN Inference (Mock): Evaluates the image for flooding, waterlogging, or landslides.
- XGBoost Cross-Validation: Automatically compares what the Vision AI sees in the photo with what the XGBoost model predicted for that district.

9. 💼 SERVICES AVAILABLE TO USERS & STAKEHOLDERS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Disaster Management & Emergency Response: Actionable real-time risk intelligence, highest risk predictions, and 16-day forecasts for proactive evacuation and resource deployment.
- Public Safety & Awareness: Live alerts, clear visual 3D maps, emergency contacts (117, 119), and flood preparedness guidelines to keep citizens safe.
- Data Export & Reporting: Downloadable CSV data reports containing district-by-district quantitative risk scores for local authorities and researchers.
- Agricultural & Environmental Planning: 16-day precipitation and environmental anomaly forecasts to assist in crop and logistics planning.
- Community Participation (Crowdsourcing): A platform for users to submit on-the-ground visual reports (photos) to assist in AI validation and provide ground truth to authorities.
