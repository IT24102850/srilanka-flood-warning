Our Mission

Reduce the initial impact of moderate flood risk, weather-related risks expanding across districts and conditions with active warnings and community support.

Real-Time Analytics

Deep dive into district-level vulnerability profiles, risk distributions, and correlation charts between rainfall and disaster likelihood.

16-Day Forecast

Integrates live Open-Meteo API data to predict future risk scores, visualized through dynamic heatmaps and interactive 2D spatial maps.

Vision AI Reports

Upload field photos via camera or file to cross-validate our XGBoost predictions with Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) ground-truth analysis.
